Overview

Dawnix Ltd ("we", "our", "us") operates the Pour — Cocktail Recipe Guide iOS application. This Privacy Policy describes how we handle information in connection with our app and this website. Our approach is simple: we collect as little as possible and keep what we do collect on your device.

Effective date: March 2026

Information We Do Not Collect

Our app does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information. We do not require you to create an account. We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, precise location, or device identifiers for advertising or tracking purposes. We do not sell your data to any third parties.

Data Storage & Privacy

Your data belongs to you. Any information created within the app — including saved favorites and custom collections — is stored securely on your device and within your personal Apple CloudKit storage.

1. Secure Syncing: Your data stays within the Apple ecosystem, syncing seamlessly across your devices via iCloud.

2. Privacy First: We (the developers) and third parties have no access to your data. All encryption and transfers are handled by Apple’s native security protocols.

3. Full Control: Your data is tied to your personal Apple ID. You can manage or remove your information at any time through your iOS iCloud settings.

Analytics

We may receive aggregated, anonymised usage statistics through Apple's App Store Connect analytics platform. This data cannot be used to identify individual users and is subject to Apple's own privacy policy. We do not use any third-party analytics or crash-reporting SDKs beyond what Apple provides by default.

Third-Party Services

Our app contains no third-party advertising networks, no social media login SDKs, and no data broker integrations. There are no in-app advertisements. All content is contained within the application bundle.

Children's Privacy

Our app is rated 17+ on the App Store due to content involving alcohol. It is not directed at children under the age of 17, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If you believe a minor has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will take steps to delete it.

Changes to This Policy & Contact

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. If you have questions about this policy or how we handle your data, please contact us at privacy@dawnix.co.uk.
